Modern Survey Equipment Explained

January 10, 2026 Tech Team 6 Min Read

Surveying has evolved from chains and tape measures to satellite-linked computers. At Earthscope, we invest in the latest tech to provide the most accurate results.

GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System)

GNSS receivers use signals from multiple satellite constellations (GPS, GLONASS, Galileo) to determine positions with millimeter precision in real-time.

Drones and Photogrammetry

Mapping thousands of acres that used to take weeks now takes hours using drones. We capture high-resolution imagery and convert it into 3D models and orthophoto maps.

Robotic Total Stations

These advanced optical instruments use lasers to measure distances and angles. Robotic versions can be controlled by a single operator, increasing field efficiency.
