About Earthscope

Precision Land Surveying Backed by Integrity & Technology.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality land surveying services that empower our clients with accurate data and legally secure property boundaries, fostering development and peace of mind across Kenya.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and technologically advanced land surveying firm in East Africa, setting the standard for precision mapping and cadastral excellence.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in every survey, ensuring transparency and legal compliance in all land transactions.

Innovation

We leverage the latest GNSS, UAV, and laser scanning technologies to deliver unmatched accuracy in our results.

Reliability

Our clients trust us to deliver precise data within agreed timelines, every single time.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to industry leader

2015

Company founded in Nairobi with a single total station and a vision to provide dependable surveys.

2018

Expanded to include GNSS and UAV services; first major road corridor project completed.

2022

Reached 500+ projects and opened second office outside Nairobi; recognized by the ISK.

2025

Launched accredited training program for aspiring surveyors and introduced laser scanning.
