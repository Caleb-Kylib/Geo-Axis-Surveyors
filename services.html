Boundary Surveys

We locate and mark property corners, re‑establish lost lines, and issue legally recognized beacon certificates. Our boundary surveys support transactions, title resolutions, and fence installations.

Additional services include right‑of‑way staking and easement verification. For complex cases, our licensed surveyors provide court‑ready plats and expert testimony.

Topographic Surveys

Our topo surveys capture elevations, contours, and utility locations using total stations and GNSS. Ideal for architects, engineers, and developers planning new construction.

Deliverables include 2D contour plans, cross‑sections, and 3D terrain visualisations in CAD or PDF format.
