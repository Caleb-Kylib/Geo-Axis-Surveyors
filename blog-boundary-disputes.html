Resolving Boundary Disputes

December 22, 2025 Surv. Martin Kimani 8 Min Read

Boundary disputes are common in Kenya and can lead to lengthy legal battles if not handled correctly. Here is how to settle them professionally.

The Role of Boundary Beacons

Boundary beacons are the physical proof of your land's limits. If a beacon is moved or destroyed, it is a criminal offense under the Land Acts. A re-establishment survey is needed to replace them correctly.

Mediation vs. Litigation

Most disputes can be resolved through mediation involving the neighbors' surveyors and the local land office. Litigation should always be the last resort due to cost and time.

Check Historical Survey Records

Old mutation forms and survey plans are the "source of truth." These documents contain the original intents of the parties when the land was first subdivided.
