How to Buy Land Safely in Kenya

February 20, 2026 Mercy Achieng 12 Min Read

Buying land in Kenya can be a smooth process if you follow the right steps. Unfortunately, many have lost their hard-earned money to fraudsters. Here is our expert guide on how to buy land safely.

1. Visit the Site

Never buy land without actually visiting the physical location. A site visit confirms the land exists and helps you assess its topography and surroundings.

2. Conduct an Official Search

An official search at the Lands Registry is the only way to confirm true ownership and see if there are any caveats or loans attached to the title.

3. Verify with a Licensed Surveyor

Engage a licensed surveyor to confirm the land's dimensions and ensure the beacons match the maps at the survey office. This prevents buying land that doesn't actually exist or occupies a road reserve.
